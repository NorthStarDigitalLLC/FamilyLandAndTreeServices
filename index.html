Our Story

Hot Headz was born in a small kitchen in DeRidder, Louisiana, where family recipes handed down for generations meet bold, modern flavors. We believe that Southern food should be hearty, honest, and just a little dangerous – hence the name.

From our crispy fried catfish to our five‑alarm gumbo, every dish is made with love and a generous pinch of cayenne. Come join us for a taste of the South that’ll warm your soul and fire up your taste buds.
