Common Questions

Frequently asked


Are you licensed and insured?+

Yes. We hold Louisiana State License #67905 and are registered with the Louisiana Secretary of State (#36054444K). We carry full liability insurance for your protection.

Do you offer free estimates?+

Absolutely. We provide free, no-obligation estimates for all tree services. Call or text to schedule — we'll assess your trees and provide a detailed quote.

Do you handle emergency storm damage?+

Yes — 24/7. Call our emergency line for immediate response. We've had crews on-site within the hour for fallen trees on homes and blocked driveways.

How long have you been in business?+

Family Land Tree Service has been serving the Deridder community since November 16, 2005 — over 20 years of trusted, family-owned service.

Do you clean up after the job?+

Always. Complete cleanup is included with every job. We rake, haul away all debris, and leave your property spotless. It's our guarantee.
